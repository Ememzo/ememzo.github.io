ADVISORY TEAM


Basic Technology Skills (BTS) Foundation Advisory board members

The BTS Advisory board is comprised of professionals from different works of life contributing from their wealth of experience and expertise in their different areas to the development of the youths

The BTS Advisory board members give their opinion when we have an ICT program/project for the youths. The advisory Board can advise on, if the program will be of benefit to the youth as planned or not Or also provide alternative methods that will ensure the youths benefit from the program. The Advisory board can also suggest programs/projects that BTS should procure and deliver to the youths
