BTS Foundation

BTS Foundation organisation is comprised of members who have been involved individually- as ACW Volunteers - in delivering ICT skills and ICT knowledge to the youths in Edo state. They have been doing this in collaboration with the Edo State ICT Agency, the Edo State Education Board and the Africa Code Week ( ACW ). ACW is an initiative of the Tech giant company SAP,  aimed at providing ICT and Coding skills and knowledge to youths in Africa. In 2018 the members were involved as ACW volunteers in running the coding program in Edo State for the first time that reached over 100 thousand(100,000) youths – in 2018 and in 2019 ACW run in Edo State the volunteer members were collaborating with the Edo State ICT Agency and the Edo State Education Board to run another coding program to youths that reached over 200 thousand (200,000) youths.

My BTS Foundation  aimes to bring ICT knowledge and general technology skills to youths of all ages for free – especially youths from less privileges background.

BTS Foundation procures ICT programs and projects, seminars and workshops, certification and specialized ICT programs that will benefit the youths especially the youths who are less privileged. The foundation aims to collaborate with ICT companies and institutions and professionals with experience from diverse areas of expertise, in order to extend their ICT knowledge and skills and trainings to the youths in all communities, pro bono


The foundation will 

  • collaborate with the state and ICT institutions and firms like SAP/ACW to ensure the continuity of programs like the coding initiative of the (ACW) Africa Code Week program – the SAP initiative, and other ICT programs from other ICT firms and institutions of benefits to the youths
  • Collaborate with other ICT programs that BST can implement and that will benefit the youths.
  • Collaborate with other ICT foundations around the globe to procure skills that are relevant for our local youths
  • Collaborate with the state and education boards to assist in creating and incorporating ICT skills into the school’s syllabuses
  • Scout for ICT programs and projects that are beneficial to the youths and strive to implement them
  • The foundation objectives will not be restricted but will be open to suggested ICT skills, certification and knowledge transfers that are beneficial to the youths and how they can be implemented
