Services

BTS Foundation offers ICT services to governments, institutions and firms to sustain BTS ICT programs to the youths for free. These services are professional services that clients can get on a discounted price and the monies will be channelled towards the objectives of BTS which is procuring and implementing skills and knowledge to the youths for free.
Some of the services include:

  • Web designing
  • Game developing
  • Digital marketing
  • Software development
  • Computer hardware services
  • ICT consulting
  • And more

Training and development provided by the organisation

  • Game Development
  • Digital Marketing
  • Software Development
